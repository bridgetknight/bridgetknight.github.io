HOWDY!

I'm Bridget, a concept artist and creative technologist based in Greater Boston, MA. I'm currently pursuing my MA in Illustration at the Savannah College of Art and Design, after completing a BS in Computer Science. I'm wholeheartedly dedicated to giving my art to the world through worldbuilding, visual development, and technical design for games and animation, while drawing on my past experiences as a software engineer.

When I'm not drawing, I'm usually outside: hiking, kayaking, birding, or foraging. I spent three months doing ecological restoration work at the Great Marsh in northeastern Massachusetts, which was one of the stranger and better decisions I've made.

I have a background in embedded systems, full-stack development, and computer vision, and I'm currently learning Blender and technical art tools to bridge that background with my art practice. Feel free to get in touch!
